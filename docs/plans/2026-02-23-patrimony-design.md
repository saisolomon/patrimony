## Overview
Patrimony is a premium SaaS for ultra-high-net-worth individuals ($10M-$500M+) who need consolidated real-time visibility across all their assets, entities, and wealth structures. Built on Hormozi's Grand Slam Offer principles.

## Avatar
- UHNW individuals, 35-70, entrepreneurs/executives/inheritors
- Dream Outcome: "See my entire empire in one place"
- Fear: Financial blind spots across fragmented wealth
- Current alternative: Spreadsheets + stale quarterly PDF reports from advisors

## Pricing
- Steward: $297/mo (10 accounts, basic insights)
- Principal: $797/mo (unlimited accounts, weekly AI briefings)
- Dynasty: $1,997/mo (family members, daily AI, dedicated advisor)

## Tech Stack
Next.js 14+ (App Router), Tailwind + shadcn/ui, Clerk Auth, Supabase (Postgres), Prisma, Stripe, OpenAI API, Recharts, Vercel

## Pages
1. Landing page (Hormozi offer stack)
2. Auth (Clerk)
3. Onboarding (3 screens)
4. Dashboard (net worth hero, allocation, AI insights)
5. Assets (detailed list by category)
6. Entities (trust/LLC structure)
7. Insights (AI briefings)
8. Pricing (3 tiers, guarantee, FAQ)
9. Settings (profile, billing)

## Aesthetic
Dark mode primary, gold/amber accents, Bloomberg Terminal meets luxury brand.
